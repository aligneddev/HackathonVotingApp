# Skill: Quality Over Quantity

Read this before starting any work on HackathonVotingApp.

---

- **Never ship more than what's needed for the current slice.** Scope creep is a bug.
- **A smaller, correct thing beats a larger, broken thing every time.** Ten lines that work are worth more than a hundred that don't.
- **Code that isn't written can't have bugs.** When in doubt, leave it out.
- **Resist the urge to pre-build.** Build what the current slice requires and stop. Future slices will define future requirements.
- **Review your own work before opening a PR.** Does every line earn its place? If you can't justify a line, delete it.
